Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 leek (white part only) or another onion, diced
  • 1 medium fennel, chopped
  • 2 celery stems, roughly chopped
  • 5 garlic cloves, roughly minced
  • 3/4 tsp paprika powder
  • 3/4 tsp coriander
  • 1 medium potato, peeled, 1.5 cm cubes
  • 1 head broccoli, (peel and chop stalk too)
  • 2 1/2 tsp cooking / kosher salt
  • 3/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1.75 litres / quarts water
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 5 cups (tightly packed) kale leaves, roughly chopped (1 small bunch)
  • 5 cups (tightly packed) baby spinach
  • 3/4 cup thickened cream
  • 1 whole juice of lemon.
