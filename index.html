Vegetable Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 leek (white part only) or another onion, diced
  • garlic, ground into past or cut long ways,
  • 5 garlic cloves, roughly minced
  • 3/4 tsp paprika powder
  • 3/4 tsp coriander
  • 1 medium potato, peeled, 1.5 cm cubes
  • 1 medium fennel, chopped
  • 2 celery stems, roughly chopped
  • 1 head broccoli, (peel and chop stalk too)
  • 2 1/2 tsp cooking / kosher salt
  • 3/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1.75 litres / quarts water
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 5 cups (tightly packed) kale leaves, roughly chopped (1 small bunch)
  • 5 cups (tightly packed) baby spinach
  • 3/4 cup thickened cream
  • 1 whole juice of lemon.

    • Method!

    Into a pot combine your aromatics (onion, garlic, ginger, and leek) add oil, and fry, then add broccoli, frozen peas, potatoes, and celery stems. Follow this with spices(paprika salt,pepper), stock, and water. Bring to a boil then add lemon, kale, fennel coriander, and baby spinach. Bring off heat and blend until smooth, cream should be added now. I add a bit of sour cream, hot sauce and chicken to mine but you can add whatever!
